Welcome to Startup Psy-Phy!

    This is an impromtu page to give you an idea of who we are and what we are working on.

    We are working on bringing elite rehabilitation therapy to the wounded warriors. We specifically selected this population because research pointed to high prevalence of PTSD that develops among wounded veterans. Furthermore we zeroed in on service members who lost limbs to ensure we are helping those who need it most.

    Idea behind Psy-Phy is to use VR to combine psychological and physical therapy. This is an expensive route, but has shown better outcomes than the therapies being used separately.
